Save memories, keep track of your mood

Thoughtwriter is a simple, elegant journaling app that uses artificial intelligence to help you stay on top of your mood and thoughts.

A built-in AI with your privacy in mind

Thoughtwriter comes with an AI that understands your mood while you write. Over time, it learns from you and becomes more accurate.

It's all on your phone, not in the cloud. So your precious thoughts stay yours and never have to leave your devices.

Stats and overviews

The mood and thoughts overviews help you understand how you feel and how your mood changes over time.

And so much more to come!

Weather and location correlation, mood swings detection, topics tagging, fine-grained emotions classification...
